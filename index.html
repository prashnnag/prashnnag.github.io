About Me

Ph.D. Candidate/Architect/ AEC/ AI Professional leveraging the cutting-edge technologies such as AI (GenAI, ML) and BIM/VDC. I have more than 6 years of research experience, more than 6 years of industry experience inside the US and overseas, and 7 semesters of teaching experience in allied fields.

I work in the intersections of AI, Construction Engineering & Management, Construction Business Intelligence, Mixed Methods Research, Resiliency, and Disasters.
