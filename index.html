About Me

Ph.D. Candidate | AEC, AI Professional | Architect

I specialize in leveraging advanced cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, especially Generative AI & Multimodal AI, Machine Learning, and BIM/VDC to transform the built environment practices. With over six years of research experience, more than six years of industry experience both in the U.S. and overseas, and seven semesters of teaching experience, I bring a unique blend of academic rigor, industry expertise, and scientific insight to the AEC domain.

I am passionate about interdisciplinary research, leveraging integrated data intelligence for next-generation built environments. Specifically, I operate at the intersections of Artificial Intelligence; Business Intelligence; Mixed Methods; Advanced Statistical Modeling with
Construction- Engineering, Management, & Business ; Human-AI Collaboration ; Industralized Construction ; Resiliency ; Disasters.
